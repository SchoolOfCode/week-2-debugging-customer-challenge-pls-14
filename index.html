Welcome to the Debugging Workshop

To open the console:

  • For Chrome: Press Ctrl + Shift + J (Windows/Linux) or Cmd + Option + J (Mac).
  • For Firefox: Press Ctrl + Shift + K (Windows/Linux) or Cmd + Option + K (Mac).
  • For Safari: Press Option + Cmd + C (Mac).
  • For Edge: Press Ctrl + Shift + J (Windows/Linux) or Cmd + Option + J (Mac).
